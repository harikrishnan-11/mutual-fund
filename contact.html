Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers before you reach out — if you don't find what you need, just message us.

Is the initial consultation really free?

Yes. Your first 20-minute portfolio review with one of our SEBI-registered advisors is completely free, with no obligation to invest.

How quickly will I hear back after submitting the form?

Our team typically responds within 24 business hours. For urgent queries, please call our support line directly.

Do you charge a fee for fund recommendations?

We operate on a transparent fee-only advisory model. Full details are shared upfront before any engagement begins.

Can I switch advisors if I'm not satisfied?

Absolutely. Just reach out to our support team and we'll reassign your account to another advisor at no extra cost.
